Real Patient Outcomes

Documented case studies showcasing successful recoveries from complex spinal and orthopaedic conditions.

Spinal Deformity

Scoliosis Correction

Patient 10-Year-Old Boy
Condition Idiopathic Scoliosis (60°)

Patient suffered from significant spinal curvature affecting posture and lung capacity. Traditional bracing had failed. Using a telescopic rod system, we corrected the curvature while allowing for future growth. The patient gained 3 inches in height immediately and returned to school in 3 weeks.

Minimally Invasive

Endoscopic Spine Surgery

Patient 35-Year-Old-Male
Technique Keyhole Surgery

A revolutionary ultra-minimally invasive technique. The patient underwent surgery for a herniated disc through a tiny incision less than 1cm. This procedure ensures minimal tissue damage, significantly less pain, and allows the patient to walk immediately after the procedure.

Conservative Care

Slip Disc Treated Without Surgery

Patient 38-Year-Old Female
Treatment Targeted Therapy

Not every spine condition requires surgery. This patient suffered from a severe slip disc causing radiating leg pain. She was successfully treated using our comprehensive conservative management protocol, including spinal injections and physiotherapy, avoiding the need for an operation.
