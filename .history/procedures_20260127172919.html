Spine Excellence

Gold Standard

Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS)

A modern approach that uses tubular retractors and specialized instruments to treat spinal conditions through tiny incisions. This technique spares the muscles from being cut, resulting in significantly less pain.

  • Tiny incisions (less than 2cm)
  • Minimal blood loss & muscle sparing
  • Walk within 24 hours of surgery

Keyhole Tech

Endoscopic Spine Surgery

The ultra-minimally invasive solution for slipped discs and sciatica. Using a high-definition endoscope, the surgeon visualizes and removes the herniated disc fragment with extreme precision.

  • Often done under Local Anaesthesia
  • Day-care procedure (Home same day)
  • No stitches required

Specialized Treatments

Scoliosis Correction

Correction of spinal curvature deformities in children and adults using advanced telescopic rod systems and fusion techniques.

Spinal Tuberculosis

Comprehensive management of Pott’s Spine through medication and surgical debridement/stabilisation if neural deficits occur.

Spondylolisthesis

Treatment for "slipped vertebrae" using fixation and fusion to restore alignment and relieve nerve compression.

Cervical Spondylosis

Management of neck pain and radiating arm pain via ACDF (fusion) or artificial disc replacement.

Osteoporotic Fractures

Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty (cement injection) to instantly relieve pain from compression fractures in the elderly.

Paediatric Spine

Specialized care for congenital spinal defects and deformities in children aimed at ensuring normal growth.

Total Hip Replacement (THR)

Ideal for Avascular Necrosis (AVN) or Hip Fractures. Utilizing ceramic-on-ceramic or poly bearings to provide a friction-free, natural range of motion.
