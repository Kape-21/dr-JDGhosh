Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh

Senior Consultant - Orthopaedics & Spine Surgery

Redefining spine care in North East India with over 22 years of fellowship-trained expertise and a compassionate, patient-first approach.

Book Appointment View Expertise

Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh is a highly respected Orthopaedic and Spine Surgeon with over 22 years of clinical experience, currently serving as Senior Consultant at Apollo Hospitals, Guwahati. He is widely recognized as one of the few fellowship-trained spine surgeons in Northeast India, bringing advanced national and international expertise in the management of complex spinal and orthopaedic conditions.

Professional Profile

Key highlights of Dr. Ghosh's medical career.

Senior Consultant at Apollo Hospitals, Guwahati (Since 2018)

Pioneer in establishing a dedicated advanced spine surgery unit in the private sector in Guwahati
Handles high-volume, high-complexity spine and trauma cases
Regular faculty and speaker at state and national orthopaedic conferences
Passionate about patient education through frequent podcasts and social media content
"Combining clinical precision with ethical decision-making."

Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh is widely respected for his ethical and patient-first approach. He strongly believes in:

  • Conservative treatment before surgery
  • Clear communication and education of patients
  • Evidence-based decision-making
  • Long-term functional recovery over short-term fixes

Areas of Expertise

Dr. Ghosh specializes in both non-surgical and surgical management of complex disorders.

Spine Care

  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS)
  • Degenerative spine disorders (slipped disc, stenosis, spondylolisthesis)
  • Cervical myelopathy and neck pain
  • Spine trauma and fracture stabilization
  • Spinal infections including Spinal Tuberculosis (Pott's Spine)
  • Scoliosis and kyphosis deformity correction
  • Spinal tumors & Endoscopic spine surgery

Orthopaedics

  • Complex hip and knee replacements
  • Arthroscopic surgeries
  • Sports injuries
  • Osteoarthritis management
  • Sciatica and Ankylosing spondylitis
  • Intraoperative neural monitoring for safety
