Procedures & Techniques

Dr. Jay Deep Ghosh uses advanced and minimally invasive techniques in both spine and joint surgery, focusing on reducing pain, blood loss, and recovery time.

Advanced Spine Procedures

Uses small incisions, tubular retractors, and specialised instruments to treat conditions like slipped disc, spinal stenosis, and spondylolisthesis while preserving muscles and soft tissue.

  • Benefits typically include less blood loss, shorter hospital stay
  • Faster mobilisation, and smaller scars compared with conventional open surgery
Know More

Ultra-minimally invasive technique using a high-definition endoscope to visualize and treat spinal issues through a tiny incision.

  • Day-care procedure
  • Local anaesthesia possible
  • Rapid recovery
Know More

For lumbar canal stenosis and some traumatic or degenerative instabilities, he uses MISS approaches with percutaneous screws and rods to decompress the spinal canal and stabilise segments without large midline exposure.

  • Aims to maintain spinal alignment and stability
  • Decreases postoperative pain and rehabilitation time compared with standard open fusion surgeries

Joint and Orthopaedic Procedures

Precision diagnostic and therapeutic procedures for knee and shoulder joint issues using miniature cameras.

  • ACL/PCL Reconstruction
  • Meniscus Repair
  • Rotator Cuff Repair

Performs contemporary TKR techniques that use pre-operative planning, refined soft-tissue balancing, and high-performance implants to restore alignment and function in severe arthritis.

  • Replacements designed for long-term durability and enhanced range of motion
  • Multimodal pain control and early physiotherapy to fast-track recovery

Utilises advanced bearing surfaces and cemented or uncemented implants to relieve pain from hip arthritis, avascular necrosis, or fracture-related damage.
